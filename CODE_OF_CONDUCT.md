# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Providing and receiving assistance in a helpful manner
- Giving credit and acknowledgment to others' work

### ❌ Examples of Unacceptable Behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal/political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Threats or intimidation

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- Project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community spaces (chat, forums, events)
- Public spaces when representing the project
- All interactions with project maintainers and contributors

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, please report it by:
1. **Direct message** to a project maintainer
2. **Email** to conduct@weather-order-checker.dev
3. **Anonymous form** via project website (when available)

Include:
- Description of incident
- Witnesses (if any)
- Supporting details
- Timeline of events

### Investigation

Reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially. Privacy of reporters will be protected unless they request otherwise.

### Consequences

Violations of this conduct will result in:
- **First Offense:** Warning and temporary restrictions
- **Severe/Repeated:** Permanent removal from community
- **Criminal Activity:** Reporting to appropriate authorities

---

## Guidelines for Maintainers

### Moderator Responsibilities:
- Set clear community standards
- Address violations promptly
- Maintain confidentiality
- Be fair and impartial
- Appeal process available
- Continuous updating of policies
